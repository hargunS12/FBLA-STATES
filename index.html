History

Blossom is a family and friend run business founded in 2020 during quarantine. Quarantine was a tough time for us all that separated some of us and brought others closer together. Not being able to see your partner in person didn't help couples. We thought to ourselves that a nice gesture like giving flowers would go a long way for people. This company started small by sourcing flowers from other businesses to growing our own.

Statistics

Being a newer business, we strive to sell upwards of 1 million flowers yearly which comes out to 150,000 bouquets sold. After paying all of our amazing employees and covering neccessary costs our total revenue annually comes out to $500,000

Environment

We at Blossom believe in a safe, loving, and genuine atmopshere where our workers and flowers can thrive. All of our members are loving, caring, and compassionate and have a passion for spreading love through our flowers.
